Top Three Movies

Fargo

Duh, it's Fargo. All time comfort movie, don't ya know.

Perfect Blue

Beautifully animated film. The story is really quite beautiful. It's really difficult to know whether the main character is truly going mad.

Deliverance

Really good movie with a solid script and excellent acting. Way ahead of its time. Gender bending rape scene, with an atypical revenge sequence. Should have been a powerhouse film.
